# Sources

Master citation list. Every clinical or pedagogical claim in this repo should trace back here. Add new sources as they are used; do not duplicate.

## Oregon law and administrative rules

- Oregon Revised Statutes, Chapter 678 (Nurses; Nursing Home Administrators; Nursing Assistants; Medication Aides). https://www.oregonlegislature.gov/bills_laws/ors/ors678.html
- Oregon Revised Statutes 433.800–433.830 (emergency epinephrine and glucagon administration by authorized personnel). Referenced within OAR 851-047-0030 as the statutory basis for emergency epi/glucagon delegation.
- OAR Chapter 851 (Oregon State Board of Nursing). https://oregon.public.law/rules/oar_chapter_851
- OAR 851-047-0030 — Delegation of Special Tasks of Client/Nursing Care. **Core rule governing this project.** https://oregon.public.law/rules/oar_851-047-0030
- OAR 851-045-0060 — Scope of Practice Standards for Registered Nurses. https://oregon.public.law/rules/oar_851-045-0060
- OAR 851-045-0062 — school-based RN scope (text not retrieved; needs reviewer follow-up).
- Oregon Secretary of State, Division 47 (Standards for Registered Nurse Delegation Process). https://secure.sos.state.or.us/oard/displayDivisionRules.action?selectedDivision=3931
- Oregon State Board of Nursing — Laws & Rules index. https://www.oregon.gov/osbn/pages/laws-rules.aspx

## National delegation frameworks

- NCSBN / ANA — *National Guidelines for Nursing Delegation* (2019). Referenced by OSNA toolkit; primary source of the Five Rights of Delegation. Not yet retrieved directly — on the reviewer follow-up list.

## Health literacy and instructional design

- AHRQ — Patient Education Materials Assessment Tool (PEMAT and PEMAT-P). Scoring instrument adopted for this project's final review.
- AMA Foundation — *Health Literacy and Patient Safety: Help Patients Understand* (6th-grade reading-level guidance for patient-facing health materials).
- CDC — *Clear Communication Index*. https://www.cdc.gov/ccindex/
- NIH — *Clear & Simple* plain-language guidance.
- Doak, Doak & Root — *Teaching Patients with Low Literacy Skills* (2nd ed., 1996). Source of SAM (Suitability Assessment of Materials).
- IOM (now NAM) — *Health Literacy: A Prescription to End Confusion* (2004). Foundational definition of health literacy beyond reading level.
- Healthy People 2030 — health literacy objectives. https://health.gov/healthypeople/priority-areas/health-literacy-healthy-people-2030
